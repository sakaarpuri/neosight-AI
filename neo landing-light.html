FAQ / The Honest Part

After the product fantasy wears off, the page should still feel useful.

These are the questions a real launch page would hide and an awareness campaign should answer plainly.

Is Neo-Sight AI a real product? No. It is a satirical concept built to borrow the language of tech launches and redirect that attention toward public cleanliness.
Why make it look premium first? Because polished products get attention immediately. The campaign uses that reflex, then turns it into a more uncomfortable question about what we ignore in real life.
What happens if I sign the statement? Your voice gets added to the public counter and state tally. The goal is to show visible, collective demand for cleaner streets and hygienic living conditions.
Why not just build a real app instead? The problem is not a missing interface. It is missing urgency, accountability, and a refusal to accept filth as normal.

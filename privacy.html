Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At VeoClara, we take privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, view our awareness campaign, or join our early access waitlist.

Information We Collect

If you join the waitlist, we collect your email address. We do not ask for your phone number, home address, or payment details at that stage.

We may also collect basic site analytics such as page views, device type, and approximate region. This information is used in aggregated form to understand site performance and traffic patterns.

On the current awareness-page prototype, some actions and counters may still run locally in your browser until a live backend is connected.

How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Notify you about VeoClara launch updates
  • Send early access or waitlist-related messages if you explicitly sign up
  • Improve site performance, reliability, and user experience

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data may be stored on infrastructure provided by trusted cloud vendors. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing privacy@veoclara.com.

If you subscribe to emails, you may unsubscribe at any time through the link included in those emails.

Cookies

We use only the minimum cookies or browser storage needed for core site behavior, analytics, and prototype functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, email privacy@veoclara.com.
